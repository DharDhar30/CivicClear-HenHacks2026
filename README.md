# CivicClear

## Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python
Backend: Node.js  
API Integration: Gemini API

## Overview

CivicClear is a civic and environmental  support platform built for anyone who may have struggles in their life or just want to help out the community, fostering a more unified world. People regularly face  important life decisions involving housing, finances, safety, and sustainability. CivicClear was created to reduce confusion, increase accessibility, and encourage responsible civic and environmental engagement.

This project was built to improve clarity, safety, and community impact on campus by helping students understand information quickly and act with confidence.

## Features

### 1. Measure (Eco Game)

**What it does:**

- Tracks positive actions you take  
- Earns points for good choices  
- Builds streaks for consistency  
- Shows your impact growing over time  

**Examples:**

- Took public transit today (+10 points)  
- Recycled household items (+5 points)  
- Switched to LED bulbs (+5 points)  
- 7-day streak of eco-actions (+20 bonus)  

### 2. Access (Civic Text Guide)

**What it does:**

- Interprets emergency alerts  
- Breaks down government policies  
- Simplifies legal notices  
- Clarifies safety instructions  

**Includes:**

- Short summary  
- Risk level assessment  
- Recommended actions to take  

### 4. Plan (Smart Planner)

**What it does:**

Tracks positive actions across all features. Every action taken through CivicClear flows into one place, allowing users to see their progress, consistency, and overall civic and environmental impact. This creates a unified system where information, action, and accountability are connected.


## Why We Made CivicClear

We built CivicClear because students are often overwhelmed by information but lack clear direction. Important civic alerts go unread. Policies feel inaccessible. Environmental responsibility feels abstract. Support resources can be difficult to find.

It turns information into understanding, and understanding into action. By combining civic clarity, environmental accountability, and structured resource access, CivicClear empowers students to make safer, smarter, and more responsible decisions.
